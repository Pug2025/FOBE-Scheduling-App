FOBE Time Clock

Use this iPad like a kiosk app. Employees sign in with a unique PIN only. Once unlocked by a manager, the kiosk stays ready on this device until an admin or manager signs it out.

Kiosk Access

The kiosk session is separate from the scheduler and is designed to stay active on the iPad home-screen shortcut.

Employee Clock In / Out

Enter the employee PIN and tap the main button. If a temporary PIN is entered, the kiosk will immediately require a permanent replacement PIN.

Enter PIN

Enter your unique employee PIN, then tap the main button below.

Optional Manual Time

Use this only when the work started or ended at a different time than the kiosk punch, such as bakery pickup or a missed punch.

Automatic behavior: small adjustments save immediately. Large self-entered changes stay visible for manager review.

Admin Sign Out

Use this only when a manager or admin intends to disable the kiosk on this device.
